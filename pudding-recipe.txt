Ingredients:
 - 200 g black rice
 - 1 ripe mango
 - 1 lime
 - 1 tablespoon blanched hazelnuts
 - 1 tablespoon coconut flakes
 - 2 ripe bananas
 - 200 ml hazelnut milk
 - 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
 - manuka honey, optional
 - 4 heaped tablespoons natural yoghurt
 - 2 wrinkly passion fruit

Directions:
 1. Cook the black rice according to the packet instructions, overcooking it slightly, then drain and cool.
 2. Meanwhile, peel and destone the mango, blitz the flesh in a blender with the lime juice until smooth, and pour into a bowl.
 3. Toast the hazelnuts and coconut in a dry frying pan until lightly golden, then bash up in a pestle and mortar.
 4. Peel the bananas and tear into the blender, then blitz with the hazelnut milk, vanilla extract and two-thirds of the black rice.
 5. Divide between four nice jars or bowls.
 6. Spoon over the blitzed mango, squeeze half a passion fruit over each one.
 7. Spoon over the yoghurt and sprinkle with the hazelnuts and coconut.
